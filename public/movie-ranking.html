The Best Movies According to Anjali

My top 3 movies of all all-time.

Mission Mangal

Is about the Indian Mars Mission and how we managed to send it in such low budget. And is about the determination and hardwork of our scientists who made it possible in half of the time.

Tumbad

This movie is about poor actions lead to poor results and the how right choice of action changes the life drastically resulting into very positive outcomes.

3 Idiots

A brilliant classic movie telling the importance of knowledge and skills
